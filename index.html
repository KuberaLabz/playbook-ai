Setup Guide

01
Create Zapier Account

Go to zapier.com. Free tier gives 5 Zaps with 100 tasks/mo — enough to demo. Starter plan ($20/mo) for client work.

02
Understand the Zap Structure

Every Zap has a Trigger (something happens in App A) and one or more Actions (do something in App B). That's the entire model.

03
Build a High-Value Demo Zap

Build this Zap to demonstrate value: New Typeform submission → Enrich with Clearbit → Add to HubSpot CRM → Send personalised email via Gmail → Notify team in Slack. That's 5 actions from one trigger.

04
Add ChatGPT to Zaps

Add the 'ChatGPT' action anywhere in your Zap. Pass data to it, get intelligent output back, use that output in the next step.

05
Use Tables & Interfaces

Zapier Tables is a database. Interfaces is a form builder. Together with Zaps, they form a complete no-code platform — not just automation.
